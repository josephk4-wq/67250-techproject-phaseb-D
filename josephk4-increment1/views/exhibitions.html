Digital Dreams: Art in the Age of Algorithms

This exhibition explores how artists use algorithms, artificial intelligence, and generative tools to create new forms of digital expression. Through interactive displays and dynamic visuals, visitors can see how machines and human creativity work together to produce art that evolves in real time.

Code & Canvas: The Language of Creative Technology

Code and visual design meet in this exhibition focused on creative technology. Featuring projections, installations, and experimental media works, the collection shows how programming has become a medium for artistic creation and immersive storytelling.

Future Memory: Archives of the Interactive World

Future Memory examines how museums preserve digital culture. Visitors can explore interactive archives, screen-based media, and restored installations that show how technology shapes what societies remember and how they remember it.
